Data Storage Notice

Your data is stored locally on your device. This app uses local storage for your profile, photos, routes, and preferences. Your data is private and stays on your device. Please note:

  • Data may be lost if you clear browser data or uninstall the app
  • Photos and avatars are stored as compressed images on your device
  • Backup your important data regularly
  • Points and favorites are synced to Firestore when online

Local Storage: Your photos and data are stored on this device only.
